# General Health and Wellness Tips

## 1. Hydration
- Drink at least 8 to 10 glasses of water daily (approximately 2 to 2.5 liters) to support organ function, skin health, and energy levels.
- Increase intake during hot weather or physical exercise.

## 2. Sleep Hygiene
- Aim for 7 to 9 hours of quality sleep per night.
- Keep a consistent sleep schedule (sleeping and waking at the same time).
- Avoid screens (blue light) at least 1 hour before sleeping.

## 3. Physical Exercise
- Move your body for at least 30 minutes daily.
- Incorporate a blend of cardiovascular activity (walking, running) and strength training (weight lifting, bodyweight exercises).

## 4. Balanced Nutrition
- Eat a variety of colorful vegetables, fresh fruits, lean proteins, and healthy fats.
- Reduce intake of ultra-processed foods, saturated fats, and refined sugars.
- Practice mindful eating and portion control.
